## 📖 About

PrepWise is an AI-driven interview preparation platform designed to solve the real-world challenge faced by students and job seekers while preparing for interviews.

The platform provides realistic interview simulations, instant AI feedback, resume ATS analysis, and personalized improvement suggestions.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI-generated interview questions
- 💼 Technical, Behavioral & HR interviews
- 📊 Real-time evaluation and scoring
- 📈 Detailed analytics and feedback reports
- 📄 Resume ATS Analyzer
- 🔐 Google & Email Authentication
- 📥 Downloadable PDF reports
- 🕒 Interview history tracking
